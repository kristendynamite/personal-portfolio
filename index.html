Howdy!
My name is Kristen. It's nice to meet you.
I'm an Epicodus student, Makeup Artist, and proud mama.
My alma mater is Santa Clara University. I was born in the Bay Area, raised in Texas, and now live in the PNW.
For several years, I worked in online advertising at Google.
In 2013, I left my job to travel and pursue a new career in the beauty industry here in Portland.
Working closely with local photographers and designers as a makeup artist has woken up my creative side, and I love sharing my vision through web design.
